Temperature

Indoor plant grow is best between 70°F - 80°F during the day.

At night, temperature around plants should not drop to less than 60°F.

The human body must stay in a much tigher range, 97.7–99.5 °F during the day. The symptoms of Hypothermia can be felt at 95 °F

Light

Measure 100 nm - 1100 nm wavelengths.

Light in the 430 to 450 nm range is blue. Blue light encourages vegetative growth through strong root growth and intense photosynthesis.

Red light is in the 640 to 680 nm range. These wavelengths encourage stem growth, flowering and fruit production, and chlorophyll production.
